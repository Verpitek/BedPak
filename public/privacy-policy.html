Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 24, 2025

1. Introduction

BedPak ("we," "us," "our," or "Service") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our Minecraft Bedrock add-on repository service.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide Directly

When you register an account or use our Service, we collect:

  • Account Information: Username, email address, and password (stored as hashed values)
  • Add-on Content: Minecraft Bedrock add-on files (.mcaddon format) that you upload
  • Metadata: Add-on names, descriptions, versions, and icons associated with your packages
  • Communication: Any messages or support requests you send to us

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

When you use BedPak, we automatically collect:

  • Usage Data: Package download counts, access timestamps, and interaction metrics
  • IP Address Information: Your IP address is collected for rate limiting, security, and abuse prevention
  • Request Data: HTTP headers, request methods, and API endpoints you access

2.3 File Storage Information

  • Add-on Files: We store the .mcaddon files you upload in our file system
  • File Hash: We calculate and store SHA-256 hashes of uploaded files for integrity verification
  • Icons: Optional package icons (PNG, JPEG, WebP, GIF, SVG) are stored and served publicly

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for:

  • Account Management: Creating and maintaining your user account
  • Service Delivery: Hosting, storing, and distributing your add-on packages
  • Security: Preventing fraud, abuse, unauthorized access, and malicious activities
  • Rate Limiting: Preventing API abuse and ensuring fair service access
  • Analytics: Understanding usage patterns to improve our Service
  • Legal Compliance: Meeting legal obligations and responding to lawful requests

4. Data Retention

  • Account Data: Retained while your account is active and for 30 days after deletion
  • Add-on Files: Retained as long as you maintain them or for 7 days after package deletion
  • IP Addresses: Retained for rate limiting purposes (typically 1-60 minutes per request)
  • Usage Logs: Retained for 90 days for security and debugging purposes

We will delete personal data when it is no longer necessary for the purposes outlined in this policy, unless we are required to retain it by law.

5. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information:

  • With Service Providers: Third-party services necessary to operate BedPak (hosting providers, database services)
  • For Legal Requirements: When required by law, court order, or government request
  • For Safety: To protect against fraud, security threats, or abuse
  • With Your Consent: When you explicitly authorize us to share your information

6. Public Information

The following information is publicly visible:

  • Username: Associated with your uploaded packages
  • Package Name, Description, and Version: Visible in package listings
  • Package Icon: If you upload one, it will be publicly accessible
  • Download Statistics: Public download counts for packages

Your email address and password are never publicly visible.

7. Security

We implement reasonable security measures including:

  • Password Hashing: Passwords are hashed using bcrypt before storage
  • JWT Authentication: Secure token-based authentication with 7-day expiration
  • Input Validation: Strict validation of usernames, emails, file formats, and data
  • SVG Sanitization: SVG files are sanitized to prevent XSS attacks
  • File Type Verification: Only valid .mcaddon (ZIP) files are accepted

However, no security system is impenetrable. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Cookies and Tracking

BedPak currently does not use cookies or tracking technologies. JWT tokens are used for authentication and are stored client-side.

9. Children's Privacy

BedPak is not intended for children under 13 years old. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If we discover such information has been collected, we will delete it promptly.

10. User Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights including:

  • Right to Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Right to Correction: Correct inaccurate information
  • Right to Deletion: Request deletion of your data (subject to legal obligations)
  • Right to Data Portability: Request your data in a portable format
  • Right to Opt-Out: Opt out of certain uses of your data

To exercise these rights, contact us at the address below.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the "Last Updated" date and, for material changes, through email or prominent notice on our Service. Your continued use constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
